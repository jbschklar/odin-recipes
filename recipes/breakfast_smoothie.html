Breakfast Smoothie

Description

This is my favorite quick and easy breakfast smoothie recipe. It's healthy, delicious, and perfect for mornings when you need something easy to take on the go.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 scoop protien powder of your choice
  • 1 cup mixed greens (super greens or baby spinach are my favorites)
  • Almond milk
  • Blender (I use a Ninja since it comes with single serving cups that fit over blades)

Steps

  1. Add fruit
  2. Add protein powder
  3. Add greens
  4. Add almond milk to fill line in a single serving container or 1 - 1 1/2 cups
  5. Blend and enjoy!
