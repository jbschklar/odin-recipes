Bulgogi Bowl

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe for the popular Korean dish, bulgogi, using easy to find ingredients. Normally this dish calls for thinly cut beef which can be difficult to find but using, regular ground beef you can get the same flavor without having to find a korean grocery with a butcher.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp seasame oil
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1-2 cups cooked white rice
  • Gochujang
  • 2 cups Chopped greens

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in a pan with chopped garlic
  2. While ground beef is cooking, combine other ingredients in a medium bowl
  3. Once the meat is browned and drained, added combined ingredients and simmer on low heat for 3 min
  4. Add the beef on top of steamed rice in bowl and finish with gochujang to taste
  5. Add some of the chopped greens and serve
