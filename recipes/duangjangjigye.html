Duangjang Jigye

Description

This is one of my favorite recipes from my time in Korea. This dish can be made with different ingredients but the base is always the same. This is a stew made with fermented soybean paste and varying seafood and vegetable ingredients. This is a version I've come to love but you can explore different varieties on your own.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup medium potato, peeled and cut into ½-inch cubes
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 4 garlic cloves minced
  • 1/3 cup chopped shrimp
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 7 dried anchovies
  • 5 tbsp fermented soybean paste (duangjang)
  • 6 oz medium-firm tofu, cut into ½-inch cubes
  • 2 green onions, chopped
